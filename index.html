About Me

Born and raised in Odesa, Ukraine in the family of computer engineers, I always thought that coding is for boys. After all, in my traditional Ukrainian family all the girls where in the medical field, and men in computer science. Being an oddball has always been my motto, so after changing career for a couple of times, I’ve decided to reinvent myself professionally in the IT field.

I have started my journey as an admin in the eCommerce department at one of the largest banks in the States. Later my curiosity helped me to move into Quality Assurance role and that’s where my coding adventures began.

Testing code and logging defects has been exceptionally satisfying. In a nutshell, you note another developer’s mistake and wait for the fix. What can be better than pointing out someone else’s mistake?!

Soon enough I realized how much effort and courage my fellow developers were putting into their code daily. And that sparked my interest to learn how to write it myself.

I have just started my journey and even though I might not be as knowledgeable and quick as others, I compensate speed with my tenacity, work ethic, and unquenchable thirst to learn.

Let’s see where it takes me!
